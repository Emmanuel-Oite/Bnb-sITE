About Us

Edaala means "home" and true to its name, Edaala Comfort cottages provide real home away from home comfort. There are three 2-bedroom cottages set within a private residential compound with a rustic theme, blending African architecture with a contemporary feel. Each cottage can accommodate 4 adults. Each room in the cottage has a private bathroom, with a shower in one and a luxurious bath tub/shower in the other. Both rooms open into a shared lounge cum kitchenette, offering simplicity and modern furnishings. The cottage has a patio that opens into beautiful gardens. You have the option of booking the whole two-bedroom cottage or just a room if the entire cottage is not required. When booking only one room, the entrance, lounge/kitchenette, and patio will be shared with guests in the other room. If extreme privacy is vital, then booking the whole cottage will offer you just that.
